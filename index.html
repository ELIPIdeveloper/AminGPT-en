AminGPT is an advanced native AI assistant designed to simplify users’ digital life. The app combines powerful AI with a simple and stylish interface to provide a unique experience of interacting with modern technologies.


With AminGPT, you can easily answer scientific, educational, and everyday questions, translate texts, find creative ideas for content creation, and access extensive and reliable scientific sources. The app fully supports Persian and English and allows adjusting response tone from formal to friendly to match user preferences.
Its minimal and modern interface makes using the app easy and enjoyable for everyone—from beginners to professionals. AminGPT can act as a personal consultant, educational assistant, research tool, and companion in daily tasks.



Key features of AminGPT include fast and precise responses, multi-language support, customization options, access to extensive resources, and user-friendly design. This app is not just an AI tool but an intelligent companion ready to help users learn, create content, and solve daily challenges.



Overall, AminGPT provides a complete AI experience, allowing users to quickly receive the best answers and solutions, access extensive scientific and informational resources, and simplify their daily and professional tasks.
